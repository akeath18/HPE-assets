Self-Determination Theory Needs Matcher

Module 12: Motivation & Behavior Change

Self-Determination Theory (SDT): Humans have three basic psychological needs: Autonomy (choice and control), Competence (feeling capable and successful), and Relatedness (feeling connected to others). Instructors should support these needs to enhance intrinsic motivation.
