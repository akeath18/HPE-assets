Exercise Physiology Fundamentals

Instructions

Drag each activity to the energy system that is PRIMARY during that type of exercise. Consider the duration and intensity of each activity.

ATP-PC (Phosphocreatine) High intensity, very short duration (0-15 seconds). Example: 100m sprint, maximum strength efforts.
Glycolytic (Anaerobic) High to moderate intensity, 15 seconds to 3 minutes. Example: 400m run, HIIT intervals, fast-paced sports.
Oxidative (Aerobic) Moderate intensity, longer than 3 minutes. Example: steady-state running, marathon, recreational activities.
