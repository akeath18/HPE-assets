Module 4: Planes of Motion

Biomechanics Fundamentals

Instructions

Drag each exercise or movement to the correct plane of motion. Understand how movements occur in different planes of the body.

Sagittal Plane Divides body into left and right halves. Forward/backward movements (flexion, extension).
Frontal Plane Divides body into front and back. Side-to-side movements (abduction, adduction).
Transverse Plane Divides body into top and bottom. Rotational movements (internal/external rotation).
